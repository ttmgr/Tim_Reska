Curriculum

Each tier builds on the previous. Tier 1 is the entry point for any organisation. Tier 4 produces internal AI trainers who can run the programme without Askeras.

Tier 1 — 4 Weeks

AI Literacy Sprint

From first principles to safe everyday AI use. Mental models for how LLMs work and fail, structured prompting, source verification, workflow mapping, and a team AI policy draft.

8 sessions 4 weeks All roles
Badge: AI-Literate Consultant
Tier 2 — 3 Months

AI Workflow Accelerator

From useful AI use to deployable workflows. Business economics of AI, use-case selection, architecture choices for decision-makers, evaluation methods, and governance frameworks.

6 modules 3 months Team leads
Badge: AI Workflow Designer
Tier 3 — 6 Months

Sector Delivery Lab

From classroom workflow design to production practice. Live sector audits, architecture and security deep-dives, pilot planning, execution monitoring, evaluation, and steering committee presentations.

6 modules 6 months Delivery leads
Badge: Sector AI Delivery Practitioner
Tier 4 — 12 Months

Train-the-Trainer

Produces internal AI trainers who can run the full programme independently. Capstone includes teaching a Tier 1 cohort under observation, building sector-specific case material, and standing up an internal AI governance function.

1 capstone 12 months AI champions
Badge: Certified AI Trainer
